OUR VALUES

WE ARE PLAYER FIRST

Everything we do is in service of players. This continuous focus inspires the most meaningful and lasting game experiences.

OUR VALUES

WE ARE SERIOUS ABOUT GAMES

Players deserve someone who cares as much as they do. We match the passion of those who see games as a meaningful life pursuit, because we do too.

OUR VALUES

WE DREAM AND DELIVER

Our vision for the future is bold. We relentlessly work to surpass players’ expectations of what games can be.

OUR VALUES

WE ARE IN FOR THE LONG TERM

There’s something special about a lasting relationship with games. Our commitment to players is measured in years and decades, not in days or weeks.

OUR VALUES

WE ARE ONE RIOT

We are player-led, not product-led. We collaborate across teams and around the world to create uniquely Riot experiences that exceed the sum of their parts.

Hyper-serve the core

We zero in on distinct groups of players who care the most, knowing they’ll bring others into the fold. As they evolve, so do we - learning what it takes to meet their needs and surpass their expectations.

Be part of the community

We meet players where they are and with empathy. Every interaction is an opportunity to add value, strengthen our relationship, and give them a sense of ownership in the games they love.

Foster a thriving ecosystem

Our games should be fair, safe, and welcoming for anyone who wants to love them. We push ourselves to design products and create environments that make it possible.

Support players everywhere

The investments we make, endeavors we pursue, and issues we address should benefit players around the world - no matter who they are, or the country or culture they live in.

Live the player experience

We immerse ourselves in global gaming culture and share the joys and pains of playing all kinds of games, especially our own. This is the best way to authentically serve players.

Appreciate the nuances

For the most passionate players, little things can feel like big things. We put thought and care into the subtle touches, off-angle choices, and reverence for the deep cuts others might easily overlook.

Bring players on the journey

We communicate openly, proactively share the 'why' behind our decisions, and don't overhype. When we get it wrong, we own our mistakes and work alongside the community to make it right.

Never take ourselves too seriously

We prefer fist bumps over handshakes. We poke fun at ourselves, appreciate a good meme, and embrace our imperfections. When we have fun, we do our best work for players.

Imagine for players

Our mission requires creativity and a vision for what’s possible. We stay curious, break new ground, and delight them in ways they wouldn’t have imagined.

Be audacious

We have the courage to chase bold dreams and make them happen. We take informed risks, iterate quickly, and enter uncharted territory with a healthy disregard for the status quo.

Hold a high bar

We would rather do less, and do it better. We ground ourselves in what will have the greatest impact, call shots with intention, and kill good ideas in pursuit of a great one.

Keep learning

We are always gaining XP from players, each other, and the industry. Every mistake is a lesson; both wins and failures help us collectively improve and evolve.

Honor and retain the core

We want our most passionate players to stick around and know that their experience will get better over time. Our continued investment earns their ongoing trust and advocacy.

Always make it “worth”

Our work should add significant value. We respect players’ time, money, social capital, and fandom by creating things we hope are worthy of their ongoing investment.

Focus on winning the game, not just winning lane

We have a long-term view and take bets that may not pay off for us right away. We work sustainably, and aren’t afraid to pivot when needed.

Treat company dollars as player dollars

We respect the money spent with us and make deliberate decisions to invest it wisely, so we have the flexibility to create a bold, new future.

Create holistically

We focus on delivering the best player journey possible, regardless of what team we’re on or the trade-offs needed. We look beyond organizational “lines” to build a cohesive end-to-end experience.

Leave egos at the door

Doing the right thing is more important than being right. We discuss, passionately debate, and challenge ideas through the lens of what’s best for players. When the shot is called, we fully commit.

Build diverse, high-performing teams

We want the best voices in the room and on our teams to help us create authentic and resonant experiences for players. We build our team comps to reflect this.

Show gratitude

We couldn’t do what we do without the community, our teammates, and everyone who helped us along the way. We never take this for granted and share our appreciation.

WE ARE RIOT

Riot Games was founded in 2006 to develop, publish, and support the most player-focused games in the world. As we went from one game to many, we have expanded to over 4,500 Rioters across more than 20 offices around the world bringing a global perspective to the games we create and the characters in them. From the streets of Piltover to the Radianite labs of Alpha Earth, we are all about making games and serving the people who love them.
